Are you ready, cadet?

NOW is your time to shine, cadet! Have you dreamt of space ever since you could dream? It's time to embark on the most significant adventure of your life and join the Galactic Expedition today!

As a cadet, you will get to live and train with your fellow cadets on Moon Station 4XT, our most prestigious and high-tech facility. Spock himself trained on Moon station 4XT!
Here you will experience the essence of space and space travel, and eventually become a space officer! Space officers will travel on the Exploration Vessel XT3297 to distant galaxies, alternate universes and alien timelines.

We work in close co-operation with the Nanoborgs and Glitchtrons to build, sustain, and develop our technologies. Nanoborgs are responsible for tech innovation and keeping our systems running, as well as medical assistance.
Glitchtrons are in charge of training our cadets to become the best pilots, fighters, medics, scientists, mechanics, programmers, and explorers they can be.

Cadet training will start with a week of intensive aptitude testing and orientation with fellow cadets. Basic training will continue for another 6 moons, honing skills necessary for space exploration and safety proceedures.
After basic training, cadets will be interviewed by our station co-ordinator and assigned a specialty, which include; pilot, commander, medic, mechanic, programmer, scientist, explorer or fighter. Other hybrid specializations are available further along in your career.

Each cadet will learn the basics of their specialization for another 6 moons, and then be transfered accordingly to our stations on Neptune, Pluto, Saturn and Mars.

Our Mission

The Space Expedition's ultimate goal is to map the universe and all alternate realities, and create a coalition of humanoids and aliens alike. Exploration is essential for survival, innovation and material acquision.

Intergalactic travel cannot be mentioned without word of our arch-enemies, the Daleks! We must defeat them at all costs, for they threaten our very existence.
Their planet Skaro houses the most valuable material in all universes, Quantum Bandwidth, which is necessary for all our operations, particularly intergalactic communications.

The Space Expedition actively works on scientific solutions to acquire Quantum Bandwidth from the sectors of the universe we control. All discovery leaders are handsomely rewarded.
